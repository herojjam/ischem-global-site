Rubber Process Chemicals

HR-908

Mandrel Release Agent — ACM/AEM Hose

Non-silicone, water-based mandrel release agent for ACM/AEM (acrylic) rubber hose vulcanization. Applied undiluted; warm-water washable.

  • Excellent lubrication, small & large bores
  • Silicone-free — clean downstream bonding/painting
  • Washable with warm water (≥70 °C)
NEWMOL-A

Release Agent — EPDM/SBR/NBR Hose

Non-silicone, water-based vulcanization release agent for rubber hose; excellent lubrication on EPDM, SBR and NBR compounds.

  • Curved & straight, small & large hoses
  • Improves yield and surface quality
  • Warm-water washable, eco-friendly
RS-620

Water-Based Anti-Tack Agent

Dust-free, nonionic anti-tack for uncured rubber — tire/CMB, calendering, sheet. Full post-cure adhesion retained; also a mold release.

  • No talc dust — cleaner workshop
  • No interference with vulcanization bonding
  • Dilution adjustable by Mooney viscosity
RC-25

Rubber Surface Cleaner & Process Aid

Low-odor cleaner that removes rubber deposits, release-agent buildup and process residues from molds, mandrels and rolls.

  • Operator-safe, no hazardous fumes
  • Non-corrosive to metal & rubber
  • Concentrated, flexible dilution
